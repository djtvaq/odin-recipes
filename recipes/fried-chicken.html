Fried Chicken

Description

This is the crispiest, spiciest, homemade fried chicken I have ever tasted! It is equally good served hot or cold and has been a picnic favorite in my family for years.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Spices

Recipe

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk
  2. Mix desired spices into flour
  3. Cover marinated chicken in spiced flour
  4. Fry until golden brown and cooked throughout
